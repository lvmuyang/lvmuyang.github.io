## Mentoring style
I view my students more as junior colleagues than mentees. You will have the freedom and independence to explore your own research interests, even if they do not fully align with my focus areas. While I do not adopt a highly hands-on approach to research projects, I guarantee weekly one-on-one meetings to provide guidance, discuss progress, and address any questions or challenges you may encounter.
